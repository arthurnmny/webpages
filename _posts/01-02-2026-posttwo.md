Great news! The planning committee for the Bainbridge Island High School Class of 2006 20-year reunion has officially begun work.

## What We're Working On

We're currently:
- Scouting venues on Bainbridge Island
- Setting a date for summer 2026
- Building our contact list
- Planning activities and entertainment

## Get Involved

If you'd like to help with planning or have suggestions, please reach out to us at reunion@tofutopia.net

More details coming in the new year!
